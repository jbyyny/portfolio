Yahoo Picknroll is an internal publishing tool for editors to program content across all global Yahoo sites.

My role: Product design lead and all analytics designs/UI.

This role-based tool allows editors, managers and editor-in-chiefs to publish, monitor, manage and promote original content, provider content and content published on tumblr. Business development managers can see how provider content is performing in the effort to manage provider costs and contracts.
