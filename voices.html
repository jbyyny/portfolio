Yahoo Voices is a responsive site that was created for creators. Built for both internal global editors and consumer authors, Voices provided easy content creation with integrated Flickr search.


Project Info/Goals

  • Publishing platform redesign for paid by assignment and unpaid contributors
  • Team of 9 engineers, 2 front-end developers and 2 product designers
  • Increase original content to publish on destination site, ad revenue, brand exposure
  • Improve content creation and collection experience of current Voices product
  • Increase original content inventory for consideration to publish on yahoo.com, Yahoo Sports and Yahoo News


In addition to posting content, users can create collections of content. Highly viewed and collected content has the potential to be featured on the main page.

Project Results

  • Innovative user content creation and consumption experience leveraging another Yahoo product, Flickr
  • Concept to production level prototype built in 5 months
  • Complete product ownership and alignment by all engineers and designers
  • Start-up size team without Product team representation
  • Project never green-lit by executives due to timing of acquisition of Tumblr publishing platform
