Guise is a mobile app concept that allows you to impulsively purchase garments or accessories anytime from anywhere. It learns your tastes and preferences over time to form the style of you. If an item cannot be found in our massive database, Guise gives you the option to find a vendor nearby or around the globe to bid on your order and fulfill your request. Happy shopping!


Process

  • My role: Solo UX, UI, product design
  • Started by writing many possible different scenarios thinking about context, device and technology
  • Researched services that offer instant purchases, the psychology of why we shop and impulse buy and personalized shopping experiences
  • Early definition of possible actions
  • Began carrying a small notebook around with me everywhere to capture notes, contexts and scenarios and new ideas about interactions
  • Generated ideas about ecosystems leveraging current technology, machine learning, big data and future technology
  • Researched RFID tags in clothing and cell phone RFID readers
  • Downloaded and explored fashion apps
  • Ethnography research in Lululemon
  • Post-its with actions to begin mapping flows and features
  • Began sketching architecture
  • Competitive audit of fashion apps and websites
  • Competitive feature comparison
  • Researched words, meanings and synonyms for brand names ideas
  • Defined brand attributes and features
  • Created moodboard
  • Refined architecture and mapped screens for prototype scenario
  • Wireframed and designed screens in Sketch
  • Created prototype of app with scenario
